About Pneumonia Detection

What is Pneumonia?

Pneumonia is an infection that inflames air sacs in the lungs, which may fill with fluid.

X-Ray Indicators:

  • White patches (infiltrates)
  • Fluid accumulation
  • Opacity in lung fields

AI Confidence Score:

Higher confidence (>70%) indicates stronger model certainty. Lower scores suggest borderline cases requiring medical review.
